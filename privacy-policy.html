We use your information to:

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Your Rights

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no system is 100% secure, so we encourage safe online practices.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and significant updates will be communicated directly.
