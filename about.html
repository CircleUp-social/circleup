Welcome to CircleUp

Your hub for meaningful connections, sharing memories, and creating circles that matter. CircleUp allows you to stay connected with friends and family in a safe, moderated environment where you can share your thoughts, ideas, and experiences in a way that matters to you.

Features of CircleUp

Create Circles

Start and join circles with your friends, family, or like-minded individuals. Connect with those who share similar interests and create a community space that feels personal and safe.

Share Memories

Post memories, thoughts, or updates that last, in a community-driven space. Your memories are safe, organized, and available to revisit anytime you wish.

Active Moderation

Our active moderation system ensures a safe and friendly environment for all users. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that CircleUp is committed to maintaining a respectful space for everyone.

Time-Locked Posts

Set time-locked posts that can be shared at a specific moment in the future. Perfect for messages, memories, or surprises you want to deliver at the right time.
